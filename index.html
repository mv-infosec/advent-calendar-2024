Flag Format

We've talked about how different CTFs have diffe rent flag formats usually. Below, we'll introduce how the flags look like in this CTF.

Flags are in this format:

infoSecClub{*insert_string_here*}

Flag Examples:

  • infoSecClub{example_flag}
  • infoSecClub{another_example_flag}

Problems 3 and 4 have different flag formats, but all the other ones should be like the model above.

Submitting Solutions

This year, we have a new system for flag submission! Below are the steps for flag submission:

  • Find the Discord Channel "bot-channel" in our Discord Server. It is located under the "Text Channels" Category.>
  • After you find the channel, type /check_flag. The bot (Flag Checker) should ask you for day # and the flag.
  • Enter the day #, and then said day's flag. The bot will inform you whether you are right or wrong!
